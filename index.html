Closed-Loop Diffusion Planning over Multi-Modal Distribution
A Novel Approach to Multi-Modal Planning in Uncertain Environments

üéØ Abstract

We present Closed-Loop Diffusion Planning (CLDP), a novel framework that addresses the challenge of planning in environments with multi-modal distributions. Traditional planning methods often struggle with uncertainty and multiple possible outcomes. Our approach leverages diffusion models to generate robust plans that can adapt to multiple modes of environmental behavior while maintaining closed-loop feedback for continuous refinement.

Key Contribution: CLDP introduces a unified framework that combines the generative power of diffusion models with closed-loop control theory to handle multi-modal planning scenarios effectively.
